Cucina
Indooroopilly.

An anthology of eats — the recipes worth keeping, where they came from, and the people who taught them to me first.

Cucina Indooroopilly is what this place has been called for as long as anyone's known me — seventeen years in Brisbane, cooking for whoever's at the table. This is where it all finally gets written down.

Cucina Indooroopilly — got a recipe to add? Send it through and it'll land in the anthology.
